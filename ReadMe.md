# 🤖 Biklo — Discord Multifunctional Bot

Welcome to **Biklo**, a powerful and fun Discord bot designed to make your server more interactive, organized, and enjoyable! 🚀
Created with passion by **Luka Labadze** 👨‍💻

---

## ✨ Features

Biklo comes packed with a variety of useful and entertaining commands:

### 🟢 Bot Ready

> The bot announces when it's online and ready to go — so you always know it's active!

---

### ❓ Simple Q&A

> Ask predefined questions and get instant answers.
> *(Note: Responses are limited and created by the developer.)*

---

### 🏓 Ping

> Check the bot’s latency in real-time.
> Perfect for seeing how fast the bot responds in your server ⚡

---

### 🧹 Clear

> Clean up your server by deleting messages quickly and efficiently.
> Keep your chats tidy and spam-free!

---

### 📊 Bot Info

> Displays detailed information about the bot:

* 👨‍💻 Developer
* 📦 Version
* 🏆 Rank
* 📈 Stats

---

### 🔗 Server Invite

> Instantly generate an invite link to bring more people into your server! 🌍

---

### 📊 Poll

> Create polls and let your community decide:

* What to do next 🎮
* Event choices 🎉
* General opinions 💬

---

### 📚 Help

> Displays a full list of available commands and how to use them.
> Your go-to guide for everything Biklo can do!

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Invite the bot to your server 🔗
2. Run the bot ▶️
3. Use the `help` command to explore features 📖
